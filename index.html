About Time Talks

Time Talks is a series of 3 webinars hosted by Professionals from the Irish Watch Industry

History

Watch enthusiasts can watch & learn (pun unintended) from expert horlogists every month about the vast world of timepieces. From high end luxury Swiss time pieces to that first digital watch you got as a kid. Our experts cover all topics relative to time (pun intended) Each month we have a different "time talk" delivered by an expert in their chosen genre.


The Why ?

Over the last two years, there has been a massive investment surge in luxury timepieces. Some watches that retailed for €8000 are now fetching over €20000 on the grey markets on sites like chrono24. Time talks were created to guide potential buyers or those interested in the dos and don’ts of investing in luxury timepieces.


What's in it for You ?

When buying something for yourself, the golden rule is to buy something you like. There is no point in wearing a watch that you don’t find visually appealing just because it’s a good investment. Time talks will guide you through finding a wearable investment that will hold its value and give you information on hidden costs like servicing.

Our Time Talk Experts


Our Fist Speaker of 2022 is Mark Stevens of Weirs Jewlers Dublin. Mark studied Business in UCD before pursuning his passion in watches. Mark joined the Richmont Group in 1999 and worked on the I.W.C and JLC brands before returning home in 2009. He is currently the Rolex Manager for Weirs. His talk will focus on the rolex brand and which models to look out for in the coming year. He also will concentrate on the grey market and why it isn't always the best option. He will aslo focus the how simple classic designs hold value. Also the how and the why of the grey market price up's and down's His personal watch favourites are the Rolex Submariner and I.W.C Pilot


Our Second Speaker of 2022 is Jane Kennedy of Keans Jewellers Cork. She is the Brand Manager for the group. Jane studied Business at UGC and joined the swatch group on their graduate program in 2001. She stayed with the swath group and oversaw the development of the Breguet and swatch brands. She joined Keans in 2014 and is passionate about customer care and seeing the fruition of investments. Her talk will focus on the Omega brand and its association with the film and entertainment industry. She will also focus on high luxury lesser know brands like Blancpain. Jane will also highlight the importance of documents and proof of ownership. Her personal watch favourites are the Blancpain Villeret and the Omega Seamaster.


Our third Speaker of 2022 is Rory Stack of Hartmans Galway. He oversees and Manages all procurement and and sales of watches for Hartmans. His talk will focus German, Japaneese and micro brands. How the Swiss don’t dominante the market to the same extent and why. He will demonstrate how brands like A.Lange and Son have creeped into the higher segment of luxury. He will also talk about examples of niche brands like Richard Mille and how traditional timepiece has evolved and why the price of these have escalated. He will also give his views on some of the best micro-brands to consider. Rory joined Hartmans in 2018 after working with the Swatch Group for four years. He is passionate about micro brands and loves researching alternatives to the traditional swiss pieces. His personal watch favourites are anything from the Glashütte Original brand
