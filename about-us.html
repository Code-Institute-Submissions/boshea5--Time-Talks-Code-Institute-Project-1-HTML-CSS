About Us

Time Talks is a series curated webinars given by professions in the watch making and retail industry. Its where wrist watch enthusiasts can watch & learn from expert horlogists every month about the vast world of timepieces. From high end luxury Swiss time pieces to that first digital watch you got as a kids. Our experts cover all topics relative to time (pun intended) Each month we have a different "time talk" delivered by an expert in their chosen genre. From retailers, collectors, jewlers, designes and manufactures they will give you insight to help grow your knowledge and collections.
